Each session is led by experienced agronomists and real Zimbabwean farm experts who understand our soils, weather patterns, and challenges.

  • Crop rotation and soil fertility restoration

  • Pest and disease management in dry seasons

✅ Day 2: Market & Money

  • Planning your planting for profit
  • Post-harvest handling and storage tips
  • Selling via digital marketplaces
  • Accessing farming loans and finance tools

Zimbabwean farmers are resilient. But in today’s world, being resilient isn’t enough — you must be resourceful, informed, and digitally connected. This training provides practical insights for real farms, from Mutoko to Gokwe, Mazowe to Gutu.

And because it’s virtual, you don’t need to leave your fields or pay for travel. You just need your phone, a bit of data, and a desire to grow.
